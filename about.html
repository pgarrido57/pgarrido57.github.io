About Me

I'm a classically trained chef with a passion for both cuisine and technology. I have a specialty in fruit carving and ice carving, and want to apply that same sense of design and discovery to digital, looking at a blank canvas and imagining what it could be under the knife or keyboard. Post culinary, I spent time in the mobility and phone sales/customer service/repair arena. I enjoy the interaction with customers, meeting their needs as much as tinkering with their phones, diagnosing and taking them apart. I'm dedicated and intense with a strong work ethic and won't stop until the job is done.

I'm also Dad of an 11 year old girl. I am bilingual (english/spanish) and hail from a Cuban-American family (and can cook a mean spanish whole pork). I'm an avid car enthusiast, and participate in groups that sponsor street racing in a safe environment, along with fund-raising for community causes. I grew up on Star Wars and the force has led me to collect over 100 Star Wars Christmas ornaments.
